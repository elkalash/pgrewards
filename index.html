More FAQS
How long does it take to receive my card?

Shortly after submitting your information for the card, you will receive an email that contains a link to your card page. Your card page is accessible only through the email we send you.

What information must I provide when signing up for the card and why?

You will need to provide your email address, full name, and a valid home address (P.O. boxes are not allowed). You must also read and agree to the Cardholder Agreement.The PGRewards Visa Rewards Card is subject to certain federal banking rules and regulations, so we are required to obtain a minimum amount of information from customers. In addition, the information you submit will be used as the billing information for the card.

How secure is the personal information that I submit?

We take the security of your information seriously. Information entered on the website meets banking and industry standards.

How do I use my card?

Your card can be used to make online purchases, like you would use a credit card, as long as the amount authorized for the transaction does not exceed the available balance. The billing address associated with your card is the address you entered at redemption; it will also be listed on your card page.

Can I add my card to a virtual wallet?

Yes! The PG Rewards Visa Rewards Card can be added to Apple Pay and Google Pay.Can I use my card in a physical store?
